# JK Brother Enterprises – Sales & Payment Tracker

A professional **mobile-first business management application** created using **Lovable**, designed for **JK Brother Enterprises** to simplify daily sales recording and supplier/customer payment tracking.

This project helps businesses manage:

* Daily sales
* Cash transactions
* Online payments
* Supplier/customer payments
* Total revenue tracking

---

## Project Overview

This application was built to support small business operations by maintaining a structured record of daily income and payments.

The app provides an easy-to-use dashboard interface with separate sections for:

* **Daily Sales**
* **Supplier Payments**
* **Customer Transactions**

---

## Features

### Daily Sales Management

* Record sales by date
* Add **cash amount**
* Add online payment amounts

  * PhonePe
  * Paytm
* Automatic calculation of:

  * Online total
  * Grand total

### Supplier / Customer Payment

* Add supplier or customer name
* Enter payment amount
* Select payment mode:

  * Cash
  * Online
* Save transaction securely

### Business Dashboard

* Mobile-friendly design
* Bottom navigation
* Clean professional UI
* Business branding support
